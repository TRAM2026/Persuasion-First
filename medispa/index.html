What's changed

Buyers stopped Googling. They started asking.

There's a shift happening to local practices right now, and most owners haven't seen it — because it's invisible from the inside. People used to type keywords and scroll. Now they ask a question and trust the answer: "best injector in Buckhead," "who's good for lip filler near me," "is this treatment safe and who's reputable."

The AI answers the way a trusted friend would — with a few names, and no menu of alternatives underneath. Either you're one of those names, or, for that client, you don't exist. This runs on a completely different system than Google, and it's new enough that almost nobody is set up for it.
